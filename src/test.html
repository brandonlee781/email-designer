LEGACY 2019 VISION

It is exciting to imagine the significant impact City First Church is going to make for Jesus in 2019. Because of your generosity over the next 12 months, thousands of people will find Jesus and experience His love, both locally and around the world.

On Sundays, December 2 and 9, City First Church will receive our annual end-of-year offering. This year, it will be earmarked towards “Legacy 2019” and, if at all possible, we are asking you to give your first month’s Legacy 2019 financial gift. You can find out more about Legacy 2019, make a commitment, or set up recurring giving at www.cityfirst.church/legacy.
